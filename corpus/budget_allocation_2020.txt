date: 2020-09-01
source: Swiggy Annual Media Plan FY2021


Total media budget for Swiggy FY2021: Rs 340 Cr. Budget has grown 41 percent year-on-year reflecting aggressive market share defence against Zomato following the latter's IPO roadshow generating significant earned media and brand salience lift.

Allocation by channel type:
- Television (linear): 52 percent — Rs 176.8 Cr
- Digital performance: 28 percent — Rs 95.2 Cr
- Digital brand (YouTube, programmatic): 10 percent — Rs 34 Cr
- Out-of-home: 6 percent — Rs 20.4 Cr
- Influencer: 4 percent — Rs 13.6 Cr

Strategic rationale: television dominance reflects brand awareness gap versus Zomato in non-metro markets. Prompted awareness index for Swiggy in Tier 2 cities: 54 percent versus Zomato 71 percent. TV weight required to close this gap within two fiscal years.

Phased quarterly plan: Q1 heavy-up on TV (60 percent of annual TV budget in Q1 around IPL) with digital performance channels running always-on. Q2-Q3 rebalance toward digital as IPL inventory premium subsides. Q4 balanced investment ahead of Diwali-New Year peak demand period.

OOH activation: 500 high-street locations across top 15 cities. Focus on hyperlocal proximity to dark kitchens and partner restaurant clusters. OOH creative to carry QR code with deep-link to app for attribution.

Budget contingency: 8 percent of total budget (Rs 27.2 Cr) held in reserve for competitive response. Trigger condition: Zomato month-on-month app download share exceeds Swiggy by more than 5 percentage points in any two consecutive months.
