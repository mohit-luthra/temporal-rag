date: 2019-06-01
source: Nexara Attribution Model Review FY2020

Current attribution model: data-driven attribution (DDA) in Google Analytics, implemented March 2019. Improvement over last-click but still limited to Google ecosystem touchpoints. Meta, TV, OOH, and influencer touchpoints not included in path.

Known gaps in current attribution:
1. Cross-channel journeys involving Meta before Google Search are undervalued for Meta
2. TV-to-Search sequences not captured
3. Organic touchpoints (direct app opens, word of mouth) not modelled
4. Cross-device journeys: desktop research to mobile app conversion not stitched in 30 percent of cases

Incrementality programme initiated: ghost ad holdout test for Meta campaigns in Pune and Surat. Results pending.

Planned FY2020 improvements: implement Meta Conversions API for improved signal, commission first MMM to provide cross-channel view, pilot matched market test for TV incrementality in cities where TV weight varies.
