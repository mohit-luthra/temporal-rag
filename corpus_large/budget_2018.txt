date: 2018-04-01
source: Nexara Annual Media Budget FY2019

Total media budget FY2019: Rs 65 Cr. First full year of organised media investment following seed funding round.

Allocation:
- Digital performance: 92 percent (Rs 59.8 Cr)
- Influencer and content: 5 percent (Rs 3.25 Cr)
- OOH pilot: 3 percent (Rs 1.95 Cr)

No television. No programmatic brand advertising. Pure performance marketing model.

Budget governance: weekly spend review against CPI and ROAS targets. Monthly reforecast. 10 percent holdback reserve for opportunistic inventory purchases.

ROI target: Rs 3.5 incremental revenue per rupee of media spend (blended across all channels, attributed). Actual Q1 FY2019 blended ROAS: Rs 3.2 (slightly below target due to TV-less awareness constraint).
