date: 2019-03-20
source: Nexara Annual Media Budget FY2020

Total media budget FY2020: Rs 218 Cr. Growth of 235 percent year-on-year. Series B funding closed in February 2019 enabling this scale-up.

Allocation:
- Television: 25 percent (Rs 54.5 Cr) — first year of television investment
- Digital performance: 58 percent (Rs 126.4 Cr)
- Digital brand: 8 percent (Rs 17.4 Cr)
- Influencer: 5 percent (Rs 10.9 Cr)
- OOH pilot: 4 percent (Rs 8.7 Cr)

Strategic rationale: television launch to close awareness gap versus Delivo. Television budget will scale to 50 percent by FY2021 if first year delivers expected awareness lift.

Media-to-revenue ratio: 22 percent. High but acceptable for category leadership push.

Risk: significant share of budget tied to first-year television creative production. Brand campaign creative agency hired. Tagline development in progress.
