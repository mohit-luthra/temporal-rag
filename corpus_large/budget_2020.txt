date: 2020-02-10
source: Nexara Annual Media Plan FY2021

Total media budget FY2021: Rs 310 Cr. Growth of 42 percent year-on-year reflects strong FY2020 revenue performance and Series C funding close.

Allocation:
- Television: 50 percent (Rs 155 Cr)
- Digital performance: 31 percent (Rs 96 Cr)
- Digital brand: 10 percent (Rs 31 Cr)
- Influencer: 5 percent (Rs 15.5 Cr)
- OOH: 4 percent (Rs 12.4 Cr)

Strategic rationale: Television dominance justified by 27-point awareness gap versus Delivo in non-metro markets. Closing this gap is the primary FY2021 objective.

Media-to-revenue ratio: 19 percent of projected FY2021 revenue. Above long-term sustainable level but acceptable during aggressive market share phase.

Contingency: Rs 18 Cr held in reserve for competitive response trigger (Delivo outspending by more than 15 percent in any 6-week window).
