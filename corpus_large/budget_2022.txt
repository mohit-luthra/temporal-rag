date: 2022-01-15
source: Nexara Annual Media Budget FY2023

Total media budget FY2023: Rs 390 Cr. Growth moderated to 14 percent year-on-year as business shifts focus from growth to contribution margin improvement.

Allocation:
- Television (linear + CTV): 38 percent (Rs 148 Cr)
- Digital performance: 32 percent (Rs 124.8 Cr)
- Digital brand: 18 percent (Rs 70.2 Cr)
- Influencer: 8 percent (Rs 31.2 Cr)
- OOH: 4 percent (Rs 15.6 Cr)

Media-to-revenue ratio target: 14 percent (down from 19 percent in FY2021). Efficiency improvement driven by MMM-guided reallocation.

Channel mix rationale: TV share declining as digital brand channels (YouTube, CTV) demonstrate superior measurable ROAS. Influencer share increasing following strong micro-influencer pilot results in FY2022.
