date: 2023-02-28
source: Nexara Annual Media Budget FY2024

Total media budget FY2024: Rs 440 Cr. 13 percent growth year-on-year.

Allocation:
- Television: 33 percent (Rs 145 Cr)
- Digital performance: 30 percent (Rs 132 Cr)
- Digital brand: 22 percent (Rs 96.8 Cr)
- Influencer: 10 percent (Rs 44 Cr)
- OOH: 5 percent (Rs 22 Cr)

Key change from FY2023: influencer share increased from 8 percent to 10 percent following evidence of micro-influencer MMM contribution reaching 12.6 percent of incremental orders, the highest ROI channel on a marginal basis.

New in FY2024: quick commerce ring-fenced budget of Rs 80 Cr within total, with dedicated MMM model. Quick commerce mix: digital performance 55 percent, hyperlocal OOH 22 percent, influencer (food category) 23 percent.

Media-to-revenue ratio: 12 percent (continued efficiency improvement).
