date: 2017-11-10
source: Nexara Pre-Launch Competitive Assessment

Pre-launch competitive intelligence for Nexara market entry planned for Q1 2018.

Primary incumbent: Delivo. Estimated 2017 media spend: Rs 120 Cr. Market share approximately 78 percent of organised food delivery.

Delivo media mix 2017: digital 65 percent, OOH 22 percent, print 8 percent, radio 5 percent. No television investment yet from Delivo.

Secondary players: FoodRun (regional, 4 cities), QuickEats (Delhi NCR only). Combined estimated spend Rs 35 Cr.

Nexara entry strategy: enter Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi simultaneously with Rs 38 Cr Year 1 media budget. Digital only at launch. Target: 8 percent share of total market spend.

Key competitive vulnerability identified for Delivo: no television presence. Nexara should consider TV in Year 2 to differentiate.
