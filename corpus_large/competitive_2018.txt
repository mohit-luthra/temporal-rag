date: 2018-12-01
source: Nexara Competitive Landscape Assessment FY2019

Competitive landscape at Nexara market entry. Primary incumbent: Delivo, estimated Rs 180 Cr media spend in FY2019. Delivo has 3-year head start and established brand awareness: aided awareness 62 percent versus Nexara 18 percent at launch.

Delivo media mix estimate: digital 60 percent, television 28 percent, OOH 8 percent, other 4 percent. Digital-heavy strategy consistent with their direct-to-consumer growth model.

Secondary competitor: FoodRun, regional player in 4 cities. Estimated spend Rs 40 Cr. Not considered a national competitive threat.

Nexara entry strategy: digital performance marketing only in Year 1. No TV until awareness base established. Outspend Delivo in Search for target keywords by 30 percent to win organic-adjacent positioning.

Share of voice at launch: Nexara 18 percent (digital only), Delivo 71 percent, FoodRun 8 percent, others 3 percent.
