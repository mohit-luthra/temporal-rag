date: 2019-10-15
source: Nexara Year 1 Competitive Review FY2020

End of Year 1 competitive review. Nexara has closed the awareness gap faster than projected: aided awareness now 34 percent versus Delivo 65 percent. SOV improved to 26 percent as Nexara TV launch in H2 expanded reach beyond digital-only audiences.

Delivo estimated spend increased to Rs 210 Cr in response to Nexara's market entry. Delivo launched an OOH blitz in 8 cities in September 2019 that Nexara did not match, resulting in temporary SOV loss in outdoor.

Key competitive insight: Delivo's Search CPCs have increased 22 percent since Nexara entered the market, consistent with competitive bidding dynamics. Both parties are now competing on the same generic keywords, inflating category CPCs for all players.

Recommendation: invest in brand search volume to reduce dependency on generic keywords where competitive inflation erodes ROAS. TV investment in H2 supporting branded Search volume growth: 18 percent increase in branded Search impressions since TV launch.
