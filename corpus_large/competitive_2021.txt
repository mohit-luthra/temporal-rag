Competitive media landscape Q4 FY2021. Delivo completed IPO in February 2021. Post-IPO capital position enables sustained media investment above profitability constraints.

Delivo estimated FY2022 media budget: Rs 310 Cr, a 48 percent increase from FY2021. IPO proceeds earmarked partially for brand building to sustain premium valuation multiple.

Nexara vs Delivo SOV: Nexara 41 percent, Delivo 52 percent, others 7 percent. Nexara holding position despite Delivo's budget increase due to superior measurement efficiency.

Delivo influencer strategy: heavy celebrity endorsement (similar to Nexara's FY2019 approach). Delivo has not yet adopted macro or micro influencer strategy. This represents a 12-18 month lag that Nexara should exploit by establishing micro-influencer relationships before Delivo.

Performance marketing: Delivo's CPA estimated 20 percent above Nexara's based on benchmark data from shared agency. Attribution to Nexara's Smart Bidding implementation and first-party audience quality.
