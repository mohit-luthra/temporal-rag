date: 2019-08-01
source: Nexara Digital Channel Performance H1 FY2020

Digital advertising spend H1 FY2020: Rs 82 Cr, 32 percent of total media.

Channel allocation:
- Google Search: 44 percent
- Meta (Facebook + Instagram): 36 percent
- YouTube: 12 percent
- Programmatic: 8 percent

Google Search highest-ROAS channel. Generic search CPCs competitive but manageable.

Meta performance strong pre-iOS changes. Attributed ROAS Meta: Rs 4.6.

MMM results: digital aggregate 41 percent of incremental orders. Search 19 percent, Meta 15 percent, YouTube 5 percent, programmatic 2 percent.
