date: 2024-11-05
source: Nexara Digital Media Strategy Q3 FY2025

Digital spend Q3 FY2025: Rs 192 Cr, 71 percent of total media.

Channel allocation Q3 FY2025:
- Google Search: 25 percent (declining; generic CPC inflation 31 percent YoY)
- Meta: 20 percent (stable post-CAPI implementation)
- YouTube: 34 percent (YouTube Shorts 45 percent of YouTube budget)
- Programmatic: 8 percent
- Regional and vernacular: 13 percent

YouTube has overtaken Search as top digital contributor for first time.

MMM results Q3 FY2025: digital aggregate 60 percent of incremental orders. YouTube 24 percent, Search 17 percent, Meta 10 percent.
