date: 2018-09-01
source: Nexara Influencer Marketing Inception Report FY2019

First structured influencer marketing programme for Nexara. Prior to FY2019, influencer activity was ad-hoc and untracked. This document establishes the baseline programme.

Initial scope: 5 celebrity partnerships, 3 macro-influencer pilots. Total budget: Rs 3.2 Cr. Platform: Instagram only.

No conversion tracking in place. Success measured by reach and engagement rate only. Celebrity engagement rate benchmark: 1.2 percent. Macro engagement rate: 2.8 percent.

No MMM attribution available for influencer channel in FY2019 due to insufficient weekly variation in spend for identification.

Key decision: commit to Instagram as primary influencer platform. YouTube brand integrations deprioritised due to production cost and longer creative lead times.

Programme governance: monthly performance review with agency partner. Creator contracts: 3-month rolling with renewal based on engagement benchmarks.
