date: 2019-11-01
source: Nexara Brand Strategy Review Q4 FY2020

Influencer marketing for Nexara relies entirely on celebrity-tier partnerships with follower counts above five million. Brand ambassador contracts with film and television personalities form the core of all influencer activity. Average cost per post in this tier: 35 to 55 lakhs depending on recent project visibility.

Engagement rates on Instagram for celebrity accounts average 0.9 percent, consistent with industry benchmarks for highly followed accounts where audience dilution suppresses organic interaction. YouTube brand integrations with celebrity partners yield pre-roll completion rates of 31 percent.

Brand recall lift post-campaign: 10 percentage points among 18-34 urban cohort. Total influencer spend constitutes 12 percent of digital budget.

MMM contribution estimate for influencer channel: 5.1 percent of incremental orders. Adstock half-life estimated at 10 days from Bayesian decay posterior. No micro-influencer activity planned due to operational overhead of high-volume creator management.
