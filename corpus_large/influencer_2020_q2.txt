date: 2020-05-15
source: Nexara Influencer Marketing Plan Q2 FY2021

Following a strong Q4 FY2020 on celebrity partnerships, Q2 FY2021 plan maintains celebrity-dominant allocation but introduces a structured test of macro-influencer (500K to 2M followers) for the food vertical specifically.

Macro test budget: Rs 1.8 Cr across 12 creators for 8 weeks. Success metric: CPE below Rs 8.0 and post-exposure order rate above 1.5x baseline.

Celebrity tier remains 88 percent of influencer budget. Key brand ambassador contract renewed for 18 months at Rs 42 Cr total commitment.

Platform mix: Instagram 65 percent, YouTube 25 percent, Twitter 10 percent. TikTok excluded from plan pending regulatory clarity.

Projected MMM contribution from influencer channel: 5.4 percent of incremental orders in Q2, slightly above Q4 FY2020 due to festive tail effects from Holi campaign.
