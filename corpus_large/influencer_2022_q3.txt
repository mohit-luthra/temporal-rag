date: 2022-09-15
source: Nexara Influencer Strategy Review Q3 FY2023

Influencer strategy has completed the transition from celebrity-dominant to macro-dominant. Celebrity partnerships now constitute 20 percent of influencer budget, retained only for major product launches and festive campaigns.

Macro-influencer pool (150K to 800K followers): 180 active creators across food, lifestyle, and regional language categories. CPE macro tier: Rs 3.8. Post-exposure incremental order rate: 3.1x baseline.

Micro-influencer pilot (10K to 100K followers) has expanded from 15 percent to 28 percent of influencer budget following strong pilot results. Early micro-influencer CPE: Rs 1.8. Engagement rate 5.2 percent versus 0.7 percent for celebrity tier.

MMM contribution: influencer channel 10.2 percent of incremental orders. Recommendation for Q4: increase micro allocation to 40 percent, reduce celebrity to 15 percent.
