date: 2022-02-15
source: Nexara Marketing Mix Model Technical Documentation v2.0

Model version 2.0 introduces Bayesian estimation using PyMC3 to address uncertainty quantification limitations of OLS. Weekly data from January 2019 to December 2021 (156 observations).

Adstock specification: Beta-geometric adstock with parameters alpha and L estimated jointly with channel coefficients. Prior on alpha: Beta(2,2). L fixed at 13 weeks for TV, 8 weeks for digital.

Posterior mean decay parameters: TV 0.70 (95% HDI: 0.63-0.76), Search 0.19 (0.13-0.26), Meta 0.32 (0.25-0.40), YouTube 0.28 (0.20-0.37).

Inference: NUTS sampler, 4 chains, 2000 draws. R-hat below 1.01 for all parameters.

Channel contribution reported as posterior distribution. TV contribution 11.1 percent (90% CI: 9.4-12.8).
