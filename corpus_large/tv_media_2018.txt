date: 2018-07-01
source: Nexara Television Market Entry Plan FY2019

Nexara enters television advertising for the first time in FY2019. Prior media mix was entirely digital performance. Television investment rationale: awareness gap in Tier 1 cities despite strong app install base. Prompted awareness 41 percent versus category leader 68 percent.

Planned TV investment FY2019: Rs 38 Cr. Channel mix: Star Plus 30 percent, Sony 22 percent, Colors 18 percent, news channels 20 percent, regional 10 percent.

Creative strategy: 30-second TVC focused on speed of delivery. Frequency target: 3 exposures per week among target demographic SEC A and B aged 22 to 35.

No MMM attribution available in first year. Brand tracking survey commissioned to measure awareness lift pre and post campaign.

Expected GRP delivery: 2,200 against Rs 38 Cr investment. Effective CPM estimate: Rs 148 based on rate card negotiations.
