date: 2022-11-15
source: Nexara Television Strategy Review Q3 FY2023

Q3 FY2023 television review. Linear TV investment Rs 70 Cr annualised. GRPs 2,800. Regional GEC now 32 percent of linear TV budget following strong performance in South India markets.

CTV investment Rs 7.2 Cr (10.3 percent of TV budget). CTV completion rate 51 percent. First CTV incrementality test completed via matched market design: CTV-exposed markets showed 8.2 percent higher order rate than control markets, controlling for baseline.

MMM update: TV contribution 10.4 percent. Linear TV marginal ROAS Rs 1.9. CTV marginal ROAS Rs 3.5. Adstock alpha revised to 0.67.

New finding: interaction effect between TV and Search identified in MMM. TV exposure increases branded Search volume by estimated 14 percent in the week following exposure. Halo effect not captured in channel-level contributions but visible in cross-channel correlation analysis.
